About Us

Founded with a passion for sharing the rich and diverse culinary traditions of Sri Lanka, ABC Restaurant has become a popular chain located in many cities across the country. Our mission is to bring the authentic flavors of Sri Lanka to the heart of every city we serve, offering a dining experience that combines tradition with innovation.

Our commitment to quality is evident in every dish we serve, made with the freshest ingredients and prepared by skilled chefs who are experts in Sri Lankan cuisine. Whether you're dining in, taking away, or having our delicious meals delivered to your doorstep, ABC Restaurant ensures an unforgettable culinary experience.
