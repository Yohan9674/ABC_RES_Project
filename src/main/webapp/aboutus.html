Who We Are

Welcome to ABC Restaurant, where culinary passion meets exquisite flavors! Located in the heart of Colombo and Around Sri Lanka, ABC Restaurant has been serving the finest gourmet dishes since 1980. Our mission is simple: to create unforgettable dining experiences through a combination of fresh, locally sourced ingredients, exceptional service, and a vibrant atmosphere.

At ABC, we blend traditional recipes with modern culinary techniques, offering an array of dishes that cater to every palate. Whether you're here for a casual lunch, a romantic dinner, or a special celebration, we promise to make every meal an occasion to remember. Our chefs are dedicated to crafting mouth-watering dishes that celebrate the rich diversity of global and local cuisine.

We take pride not only in our food but in creating a welcoming environment that feels like home to our guests. Join us at ABC Restaurant, where food, friends, and family come together to celebrate the joy of eating well.
